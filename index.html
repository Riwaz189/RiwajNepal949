About NEB

The National Examination Board (NEB) is the primary governing body for school-level examinations in Nepal. It was established to enhance the quality, reliability, and credibility of academic assessments. NEB conducts examinations for Grade 11 and 12, setting a standard curriculum and maintaining uniformity across educational institutions. It plays a vital role in shaping the academic future of students by offering structured guidelines, fair evaluation systems, and certifications that are recognized nationwide.
